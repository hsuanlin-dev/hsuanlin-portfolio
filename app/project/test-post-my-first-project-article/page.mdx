## Why I made this test post

Before adding all seven of your real articles, it helps to verify that one sample post can:

- appear in the blog page
- open with the correct URL
- display the cover image
- render all markdown content without errors

This helps you avoid repeating the same formatting issue seven times.

## What to check on the page

When you open this article locally, confirm the following:

1. The page loads without a compile error.
2. The title shows correctly.
3. The cover image appears.
4. The content sections render in order.
5. The URL matches the folder name.

> If this article works, you can duplicate the same structure for your other posts.
