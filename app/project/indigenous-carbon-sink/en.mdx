# Indigenous Carbon Sink — ESG Matchmaking Platform

I served as the **Senior Design Engineer (Visual & Narrative Lead)** for the Indigenous Carbon Sink platform, a project launched at the **Legislative Yuan**. This initiative bridges the gap between **National Taipei University's** scientific research, corporate ESG goals, and the ancestral wisdom of indigenous tribes, creating a national-scale digital anchor for sustainable carbon-trading.

## Project Overview

The Indigenous Carbon Sink platform is a **national-level ESG matchmaking hub** that connects three worlds — academia, government policy, and indigenous land stewardship. My role spanned the full creative and technical lifecycle, from cultural visual research and brand storytelling to frontend implementation.

Core responsibilities include:

- Conducting deep visual research across 16 indigenous tribes to define the brand identity
- Independently authoring the brand narrative and editorial copy for the entire platform
- Implementing a performance-first frontend architecture optimized for maximum reach
- Achieving 100% design fidelity across mobile and desktop environments

## The Challenge

**The "Authority vs. Warmth" Paradox:** How to balance the rigorous data requirements of academic and corporate sectors with the organic, temperature-rich narrative of indigenous land preservation.

**Maximizing Accessibility & Reach:** Ensuring that this high-stakes advocacy platform achieves **"Zero-Friction Access"** across all devices and network environments, especially for public policy stakeholders.

## Strategic Solution

I deconstructed the project into a holistic **"Visual, Narrative, and Code"** strategy:

- **Evidence-Based Visual Identity:** Conducted deep visual research into **16 indigenous tribes**. I identified the "Sun" as a universal symbol of life and guardianship, modernizing it into a logo that represents the symbiosis of academia, government, and tribes.
- **Editorial Content Strategy:** I independently authored the brand storytelling and copy. By collaborating with the Taipei University research team, I translated dense carbon metrics into a **compelling, editorial-style digital narrative**.
- **Performance-Driven Minimalism:** Instead of heavy modern frameworks, I intentionally chose a **"Zero-Dependency" approach** using Bootstrap and Vanilla JS. This strategic decision ensured maximum device compatibility and "instant-load" performance for a high-impact public launch.

## Key Contributions

- **Full-Stack Ownership:** Owned the entire creative and technical lifecycle — from visual research and brand definition to copywriting and frontend implementation.
- **Strategic Engineering:** Demonstrated the professional judgment to prioritize **Access Velocity** and **Compatibility** over tech-stack complexity for a public-facing advocacy tool.
- **Quality Gatekeeping:** Achieved 100% design fidelity while ensuring the complex data charts load seamlessly across mobile and desktop environments.
